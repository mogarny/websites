Continental GT

The story

Royal Enfield released the Continental GT at the peak of the original cafe racer craze. They saw a gap in the market for a good-looking, learner-legal motorcycle that was a decent performer and had an affordable price tag. During the development of the Continental GT 250, it was rumoured that Enfield invited young apprentices to contribute their ideas during the design process.
